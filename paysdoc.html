Brand Voice Guidelines

‚úÖ DO's

  • Be authentic: Speak like a fellow restaurant owner
  • Be confident: Back claims with real data
  • Be supportive: Understand restaurant challenges
  • Be clear: Avoid technical jargon
  • Be urgent: Emphasize cost of inaction
  • Be proof-driven: Always include evidence

‚ùå DON'T's

  • Don't oversell: Avoid pushy sales language
  • Don't generic: Avoid one-size-fits-all messaging
  • Don't negative: Don't attack competitors directly
  • Don't complex: Avoid technical specifications
  • Don't promise: Avoid unrealistic guarantees
  • Don't ignore: Address concerns directly
